Every computer part is somehow connected and work together to make a pc. A motherboard is that green rectangle with all of the connectors and fans on it.It connects all of the component s together and gives power to the brains of the machine. Speaking of the brains of the machine , that is called the processor.The photo of the processor is a little green square that has a lot of pins on the back.The processor takes binary code and adds, subtracts, and multiplies it in order to save data.Ram stands for random access memory.The ram of a computer stores data and information.The hardrive is a data storage device used for storing and retrieving digital information using rapidly rotating disks (platters) coated with magnetic material.A power supply is a big block looking thing. It converts power from the wall outlet to usable energy for the pc's components. Computer components communicate with eachother through a "bus" on the motherboard. In order to display what your computer is viewing you need a monitor. A monitor is basically a tv that is small and can only connect to a pc or gaming console. In order to control what you are viewing on the monitor, you need a mouse and keyboard. a mouse is used to select and move objects while the keyboard is used to type words or phrases.
